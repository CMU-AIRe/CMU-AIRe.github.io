CMU Artificial Intelligence and Reinforcement Learning (AIRe) Lab

AIRe Lab @ CMU

Welcome to the AIRe lab website! Our research focus is to learning methods that enable machines to act intelligently and autonomously open-world scenarios. To this end, we build reinforcement learning and sequential decision-making algorithms for building AI systems, and study intersections with several domains (e.g., language, robotics, software agents, applications in science and engineering).
